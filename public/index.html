    • FRAT is 3D Virtual Classroom with attendance based on facial recognition and many other features designed to act as a platform for conducting online classes.
    We use "One class One room concept" where students need not go through the difficulty of shifting rooms from one session to another. They stay in one room throughout their semester. In other words, students belonging to a section are given a link that is used by the faculty to enter and leave in their respective timings.

    Our Work

    Our Work

    • 3D virtual classroom -

    • Calender - Schedule and manage classes by using the calender where you can view, organise and create events.

    • Online Quizzes - Any number of quizzes can be conducted during the session with just a click.

    • Whiteboard Support - Insert shapes, draw, write text with the help of various tools provided while teaching without any difficulty. This functionality is restricted only to teachers and students can simply view the whiteboard. The whiteboard image can be easily saved as a PDF file locally to your device. Nothing is collected or stored by us and is deleted once the class is closed.

    • Smart Attendance - FRAT provides an AI powered face recognition solution which uses Computer Vision and Machine Learning algorithms to mark the attendance of the students of the organisation.

      The System records the attendance by clicking pictures periodically and keeping a score. A student is marked attendance if this score meets a threshold.
